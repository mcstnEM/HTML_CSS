About Grumpy Cat

The Story Behind Grumpy Cat

Our journey began with a single photograph that sparked a global phenomenon. Grumpy Cat, whose real name was Tardar Sauce, captured the hearts and imaginations of millions with her perpetual frown and sassy demeanor. Born in April 2012, this little feline's unique expression, due to feline dwarfism, quickly catapulted from a familial amusement to an internet sensation overnight. What initially started as shared photos among friends and family burgeoned into an expansive online community, dedicated to all things Grumpy Cat. This website serves as a tribute and a central hub for fans worldwide to celebrate her legacy, share laughter, and continue spreading the joy that Grumpy Cat brought to so many lives.

What Grumpy Cat Offers

Expanding from its humble beginnings, the Grumpy Cat brand now encompasses a wide array of products and content designed to bring a smile to even the grumpiest faces. Our official merchandise, including a bespoke line of clothing, accessories, and the ever-popular Grumpy Cat plush toys, captures the essence of our beloved mascot's spirit. Beyond tangible goods, we offer an extensive collection of digital content ranging from heartwarming to humorous stories about Grumpy Cat's adventures, insightful articles on pet care, and an engaging community platform. Here, fans can connect, share their own grumpy pets, and participate in exclusive Grumpy Cat events. It's a celebration of the joy that our pets, regardless of their mood, bring into our lives.
